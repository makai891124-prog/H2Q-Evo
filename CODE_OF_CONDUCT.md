# Code of Conduct

## 我们的承诺 (Our Commitment)

为了营造一个开放和欢迎的环境，作为贡献者和维护者，我们承诺：

为了我们社区中的每个人，无论其年龄、体型、残疾、种族、性别认同与表达、经验水平、国籍、个人外表、宗教或性认同与性取向如何，都能提供一个无骚扰的体验。

*In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we as contributors and maintainers commit to making participation in our project and our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.*

---

## 我们的标准 (Our Standards)

建立积极环境的行为示例包括：

- 使用欢迎和包容的语言
- 尊重持不同观点和经历
- 接受建设性批评
- 关注对社区最有利的事情
- 表现对其他社区成员的同情

*Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:*

- *Using welcoming and inclusive language*
- *Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences*
- *Gracefully accepting constructive criticism*
- *Focusing on what is best for the community*
- *Showing empathy towards other community members*

不可接受的行为示例包括：

- 使用带有性内涵的语言或意象
- 人身攻击、侮辱或贬低评论
- 公开或私下骚扰
- 未经明确同意发布他人的隐私信息（如住址或电子邮件地址）
- 其他可能合理地被视为不专业或不受欢迎的行为

*Examples of unacceptable behavior by participants include:*

- *The use of sexualized language or imagery and unwelcome sexual attention or advances*
- *Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks*
- *Public or private harassment*
- *Publishing others' private information, such as a physical or electronic address, without explicit permission*
- *Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting*

---

## 执法 (Enforcement)

### 报告问题 (Reporting Issues)

社区领导者负责澄清可接受行为的标准，并应对任何不可接受行为的报告采取适当的纠正措施。

*Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying the standards of acceptable behavior and are expected to take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any instances of unacceptable behavior.*

### 报告渠道 (Reporting Channel)

如果您目睹或经历了不可接受的行为，请通过以下方式报告：

1. **GitHub Issues**: 创建私密问题 (在 "Security" 部分)
2. **Email**: [your-email@example.com]
3. **Direct Message**: 通过 GitHub 私信维护者

所有报告都会被认真对待和保密处理。

*If you are subject to or witness unacceptable behavior, or have any other concerns, please notify a project maintainer as soon as possible.*

---

## 执行 (Enforcement)

### 后果 (Consequences)

不遵守本行为准则的社区成员可能会面临暂时或永久的后果，包括：

1. **警告**: 对不当行为的正式警告
2. **临时禁止**: 在指定期间内从社区活动中移除
3. **永久禁止**: 从项目和社区中永久移除

*Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project team at [contact information]. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated and will result in a response that is deemed necessary and appropriate to the circumstances.*

### 申诉程序 (Appeals)

如果您认为禁止决定不公平，可以通过向 [email] 发送详细说明来申诉。

*If a participant believes they have been treated unfairly, they may appeal the decision by contacting [maintainers].*

## 社区准则 (Community Guidelines)

### 好的参与方式 ✅

- 积极和建设性的反馈
- 尊重不同的观点
- 共享知识和经验
- 支持新成员融入社区
- 庆祝他人的成就

### 不当行为 ❌

- 骚扰或欺凌
- 引发冲突的言论
- 垄断讨论
- 诋毁他人工作
- 发表仇恨或歧视性言论

感谢每一位为营造积极、包容和支持的社区环境而努力的成员。

*Thank you for being part of a positive, inclusive, and supportive community.*
